About Us

filigranduo brings together the expressive artistry of violin and piano in a dynamic partnership nurtured at the Maastricht Conservatoire. United by a shared passion for both classical masterpieces and contemporary gems, the duo crafts finely woven performances that explore colour, texture and narrative.

From intimate recitals to large concert stages, filigranduo curates programmes that invite audiences on a journey through time and style – shimmering baroque sonatas, fervent romantic showpieces, and vibrant new works from living composers.
