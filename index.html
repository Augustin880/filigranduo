About Us

Theodore Kim and Seulbi Cho are two students from the Conservatorium of Maastricht who formed a violin and piano duo in May 2024. The duo takes its name from the German word ‘Filigran’, which means very delicate. A term that reflects their musical pursuit.

They enjoy performing diverse styles of music, with a particular love for classical traditions. Their performances aim to create an emotional connection with audiences through sincere and expressive interpretations. As emerging musicians, Theodore and Seulbi are committed to growing both individually and as a duo, sharing their passion for music with every performance.
