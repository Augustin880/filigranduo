About Filigran Duo

Discover the story, passion, and artistry behind our music.

Filigran Duo is the culmination of a shared musical vision between two dedicated artists. Our partnership is built on a foundation of mutual respect, a deep love for the chamber music repertoire, and a relentless pursuit of expressive, authentic performances that connect with audiences on a profound level.

The Musicians

Theodore Kim

The Violinist

A graduate of the Juilliard School, Elara has captivated audiences worldwide with her virtuosic playing and profound musicality. Her performances are celebrated for their emotional depth and technical brilliance, earning her accolades in numerous international competitions.

Seulbi Cho

The Pianist

Liam is an alumnus of the Curtis Institute of Music, renowned for his sensitive and collaborative approach to chamber music. His nuanced interpretations and powerful command of the piano make him a sought-after partner for many of today's leading instrumentalists.

Our Journey Together

2018: A Fateful Meeting

Elara and Liam first crossed paths at a summer music festival in Aspen, where a last-minute pairing for a Brahms sonata revealed an instant and undeniable musical chemistry.

2019: Debut Concert

Filigran Duo was officially formed, making their critically acclaimed debut at Carnegie's Weill Recital Hall with a program celebrating the works of French composers.

2022: First Album Release

The duo released their debut album, "Echoes & Reflections," which was praised by critics for its "impassioned dialogue" and "exquisite craftsmanship."
